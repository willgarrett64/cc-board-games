CC Board Games

What We're All About

Being a board game lover myself, I always found myself looking forward to Christmas to get the family together and finally get to play some games. However, it was always short-lived as after just a day or two of being together, I head back home and I'm back to not knowing when will the next time will be that I get to play.

This lead to CC Board Games being born - a club for board game lovers from around the country to get together, meet up and play. We mix things up, playing some of the favourites as well as some of the lesser-known ones.

Given the coronavirus pandemic, we've had to adapt and found inventive ways to play online until things get back to relative normailty and we can all meet up in person again.
